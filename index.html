The Rules

How It Works

Three simple steps to become a Cinemystry champion.

🎬

A movie title is hidden

Each round shows a movie title as blank tiles. Some letters are revealed to get you started.

🔤

Guess letters one by one

Press a key or tap the on-screen keyboard. Wrong letters cost you a life — you only have 3.

🏆

Score, streak & win

Guess faster and without hints for maximum points. Build streaks for bonus multipliers.

Features

Why Cinemystry?

Every detail is designed to make guessing movies ridiculously fun.

Real Movie Data

Thousands of movies from English and Hindi cinema, curated for a challenging but fair experience.

Dynamic Scoring

Points decay with time and mistakes. Go fast, stay perfect, and unlock speed bonuses for massive scores.

Strategic Hints

Reveal Year, Genre, or a random Letter. But every hint chips away at your potential score — choose wisely.

Countdown Timer

Optional timer mode adds pressure. The faster you guess, the higher the speed bonus you unlock.

Streak Multipliers

Win consecutive rounds to build streaks. Chain 3+ wins for auto bonus point dumps on top of your score.

Local Leaderboard

Every game you play is saved locally. Track your progress, beat your personal best, and compare difficulties.
