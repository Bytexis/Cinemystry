Master the Mystery

Cinemystery is more than just a quiz—it's a race against time and your own movie memory. Whether you're playing alone or challenging a friend, here's everything you need to know to reach the top of the leaderboard.

1

The Objective

A movie title is hidden behind tiles. Your goal is to reveal the correct title by guessing letters. Every correct guess brings you closer to victory, while every wrong guess costs you a life.

2

Scoring System

Points are awarded based on speed and difficulty. Each letter correctly guessed adds to your potential round score. Guessing the full title with fewer mistakes earns you a significant bonus.

3

Using Hints

Stuck? You can use up to three hints per movie. You can reveal the Release Year, the Genre, or Reveal a Random Letter. Be careful—each hint permanently reduces your maximum potential score for that round.

4

Hearts & Lives

In single-player mode, you start with 3 hearts. A wrong letter guess consumes one heart. If you lose all hearts, the game is over and your current streak resets.

5

Multiplayer Battles

Challenge a friend locally! Players take turns guessing letters. If a player makes a mistake, the turn passes. If you guess the final letter, you "steal" the round points. Strategy is key!

Pro Tips for High Scores

  • Common vowels are a safe start, but consonants like 'S', 'T', and 'R' appear frequently in titles.
  • The Release Year hint is often the most powerful way to narrow down your search.
  • Building a streak (consecutive wins) provides a massive multiplier to your global rank.
  • Don't rush! A wrong guess is more penalizing than taking an extra few seconds to think.
