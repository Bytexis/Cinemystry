📖 Game Guide

Master the Mystery

Cinemystry is more than a quiz — it's a race against time and your own movie memory. Here's everything you need to reach the top of the leaderboard.

The Rules

1

A movie title is hidden

Each round reveals a movie title as blank tiles. One or two letters are pre-filled to get you started. Your goal: expose the full title before losing all your hearts.

2

Guess letters one by one

Press a key on your physical keyboard or tap the on-screen keyboard. A correct letter reveals all matching tiles. A wrong letter costs you one ❤️ — you only have 3.

3

Earn points and build streaks

Successfully guess a movie and earn points. Win consecutive rounds to build a streak — streaks grant large bonus point dumps and track your skill. A wrong heart reset ends your streak.

4

Use hints strategically

Stuck? Use up to 3 hints per round — reveal the Release Year, the Genre, or a random letter. Be careful: each hint permanently reduces the maximum points you can earn that round.

5

Multiplayer: take turns to steal

In local multiplayer, two players take turns guessing letters. If you guess wrong, the turn passes to your opponent. The player who correctly reveals the last letter wins that round's full point value — strategy is everything!

🔥 Pro Tips for High Scores

  • Start with common consonants — S, T, R, N — they appear in almost every movie title.
  • The Release Year is the most powerful hint. It narrows down the era immediately.
  • Streaks matter more than single scores. 3 wins in a row often beats one perfect round.
  • Don't rush into a guess — one wrong letter is more penalizing than pausing to think.
  • In multiplayer, let your opponent go first on a tough movie — let them waste hints and hearts.
  • Shorter movie titles (3–5 letters) are often harder — fewer chances to guess before a wrong letter.
